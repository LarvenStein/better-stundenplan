<!-- Features -->
<p><strong>Features:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Week View: Displays the schedule for an entire week.</li>
  <li>Day View: Displays the schedule for a single day.</li>
  <li>Detail View: Shows details of individual lessons and allows setting aliases for subjects, teachers, and rooms.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Note:</strong> This app is only available in German.</p>
